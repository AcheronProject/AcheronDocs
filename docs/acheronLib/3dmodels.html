The 3D models¶

Usage¶

These models are used for the sole purpose of making renders for mechanical compliance checking. They are not used to make or manufacture any kind of product.

Description¶

  • 4PinHeader.step is self-explanatory;

  • angle4PinHeader.step os a 90-degree angled 4-pin header. It was taken from this link (accessed may 20, 2019);

  • 5021_XTAL.step describes a 50-by-32 mil oscillator crystal. This footprint was taken from this link (accessed may 12, 2019) and improved by myself (added colors, some fillets).

  • ALPS_EC11E.step describes the ALPS EC11E rotary encoder. It was taken from the official page (accessed may 12, 2019) and slightly modified by myself (added colors and materials).

  • MDBT40.step describes the bluetooth module MDBT40. It was taken from this link (accessed may 12, 2019).

  • TYPE-C-31-M-12.step describes the USB type C connector I generally use. It was made by myself.

  • Tactile_SMD-PushBtn_5x5mm.step describes the SMD push button I generally use. It was taken from this link (accessed may 12, 2019).

  • WS2812.stp describes the RGB LED WS2812. It was taken from this link (accessed may 12, 2019).

  • kailh_socket_mx.stp describes the Kailh Hotswap socket. It was taken from the OLKB qmk_hardware repository (accessed may 12, 2019).

  • mx_switch.step describes an MX-style keyswitch. Taken from this link.
